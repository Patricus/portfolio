About Me

Luck is when opportunity meets preparedness.

Through out my life, I've always been the tech guy. Helping friends and family with computers is a core piece of my childhood. It has instilled a strong drive for teamwork and a health culture.

Working in retail, I've learned many ways to communicate. Wether with coworkers, managers, or the customer, I can conduct myself to create a strong working environment and happy customer satisfaction.

Naturally curious, I will always be learning and adapting new methods and technologies to my design and workflow. A big reason for my draw to the tech industry is it rapid pace and continual improvement. I can never learn enough and that's invigorating!

Dedication to ones craft is necessary, luck for me I'm doing what I love. Computers have been a life long passion for me. Figuring out how they work, and how to make them work better, gives me a euphoria that little else can compare too. This is magnified when I get to share it with a team.
